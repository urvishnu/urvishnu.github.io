About

Academic Excellence & Research Leadership

Dr. Y V Srinivasa Murthy is a distinguished Associate Professor, Academic Coordinator for Post Graduate programs, and NBA Coordinator at the School of Computing and Engineering at Manipal Institute of Technology Bengaluru, part of the prestigious Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), recognized with Institute of Eminence status by the Government of India.

Born in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, Dr. Murthy has established himself as a leading researcher in the fields of signal processing, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. He earned his PhD from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) Surathkal in 2019, conducting groundbreaking research in Content-based Music Information Retrieval (CB-MIR) under the supervision of Dr. Shashidhar G. Koolagudi.

With a rich academic career spanning multiple prestigious institutions including VIT Vellore, NITK Surathkal, GITAM University, and ANITS, Dr. Murthy brings extensive experience in teaching, research, and academic administration. His passion for education has been recognized with the Best Teacher Award for excellence in teaching.
